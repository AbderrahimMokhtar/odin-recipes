Baklava

Description

Baklava (also spelled baklawa in many Arab countries) is a rich, sweet, and flaky layered pastry dessert that is a beloved classic across the Arab world, the broader Middle East, and Mediterranean regions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • ¼ lemon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ pounds chopped almonds
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 ½ cups unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen phyllo pastry, thawed
